## Differentiation

The key difference between plans is **sales tools**:

- **Esencial** = Pure microsite. Everything the buyer sees — gallery, videos, maps, tours, contact form, availability. For developers who just need a beautiful digital presence and handle sales/quotations externally.

- **Profesional** = Microsite + sales engine. Adds the cotizador (PDF quotation generator), advanced analytics, custom domain, and team collaboration. For developers who want to manage their full sales workflow inside NODDO.

- **Enterprise** = Profesional + custom tools. For developers with 10+ active projects who need custom-built tools, dedicated onboarding, and premium 24/7 support. Pricing is personalized via sales.

## Billing

- Monthly or annual billing
- Annual saves 20% (~$63/mo for Esencial, ~$119/mo for Profesional)
- 14-day money-back guarantee
- No contracts, cancel anytime
